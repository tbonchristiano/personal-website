About Me

I was born and raised in Brazil, find a piece of my identity in my Italian heritage, and realized my appreciation and passion for immersing myself in different cultures while living in Spain and the United States. My multinational background defines me. Creating long lasting and genuine relationships with people from all around the world fuels my interest and curiosity in learning from others and their diverse backgrounds and experiences.

Some of my interests include traveling, sports and languages.
• Traveling: every year, when choosing where to spend my vacations, a new and different location is always top of mind. So far, I have been to 19 countries and I'm eager to visit many more. Currently, my new top countries to visit are: Morroco, South Africa, Egypt, India and Japan.
• Sports: I follow an active lifestyle and enjoy working out, hiking, running, and learning new sports. I have been practicing water polo for over ten years and had the opportunity to play at both a national and professional level, representing my home country at the South American championship (more about it in the Sports section). Currently, I participate of a water polo masters team, a running club, and an adult volleyball sports league.
• Languages: my multinational background has taught me the appreciation of languages. Being able to communicate with someone with their first language is always more impactful. I'm fluent in Portuguese and English, and have a proficient level in Spanish. My two main goals at the moment are to improve my Spanish and to learn Italian.
I'm a huge believer of (i) being drastically open minded, (ii) being able to figure things out in any possible way, (iii) being my best self at all times and in any situation.

ps: regarding my belief (ii) above, this website is a great example of figuring it out - I decided to learn how to code a website and, through free courses and Youtube videos, I learned how to write with html, css and javascript languages and built this website from the ground up.

Sports

Since my childhood I have always had an active lifestyle. When I was a kid, I played several team sports such as basketball, handball, volleyball, and, as a true Brazilian, soccer. Right before becoming 12 years old, I learned about water polo and joined my sports club team. My last soccer position was as a goalie, so I also saw this new sport as an opportunity to play in the field and score some goals, but... You guessed it right, within my second practice I was already the new goalie in our water polo team. I ended up loving being a water polo goalie and never stopped playing the sport. Recently, I joined the Nashville Channels Cats masters team, where I practice once to twice a week and play tournaments throughout the year.
